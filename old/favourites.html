My Favourite Things on the Internet

A collection of blog posts, webpages, etc that inspire me, and that make me more optimistic for the future.

  • Duty of Risk by Jake Cooper

    This post helped get me through school, and shaped much of how I approach career & work. A constant reminder to be more ambitious.

  • Why we can't have nice software by Andrew Kelley

    Helps me appreciate great software that has stood the test of time. That's the type of software I want to build.

  • The curse of smart people by Avery Pennarun

    The reason why I don't want to work at a large, successful company.

  • The friendship that made Google huge by James Somers

    Explores the lives of two legendary programmers I deeply admire and look up to.
